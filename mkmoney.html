Grey Area Hustles

What You Should Know

  • Ticket Reselling: Profitable, but laws vary—know your local limits.
  • Bot Arbitrage: Snagging drops is effective, but against many site rules.
  • Account Flipping: Risky—platforms can permanently ban resold accounts.
  • Ghostwriting: Dubious ethics and potential academic blowback.
  • Unlicensed Services: Avoid trades that require certification without proper credentials.
  • Crypto/NFT Pump & Dump: May ride the edge of legality—proceed with caution.
  • Cash Gigs Without Reporting: Common—but the CRA doesn’t play.
  • “Guru” Courses Without Proof: If it feels too slick, it may cross the line into false advertising.
