Lawn Care Usage Practice

Guidelines for applying products and staying within recommended usage variance.

Product Application

  • Read and follow all instructions on the product label.
  • Measure your lawn area to determine the correct amount of product needed.
  • Use appropriate equipment for even application.
  • Apply products during recommended weather conditions.
